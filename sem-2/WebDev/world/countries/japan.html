Japan

Japan is an island nation in East Asia consisting of four main islands and thousands of smaller ones. Known for harmoniously blending ancient traditions with modern innovation, Japan is a global leader in technology, automotive manufacturing, and robotics. The country has a rich cultural heritage including tea ceremonies, martial arts, and traditional arts like calligraphy and origami. Tokyo, its capital, is one of the world's most populous metropolitan areas and a hub of fashion, technology, and cuisine. Japan is famous for its cherry blossoms, Mount Fuji, bullet trains, anime and manga culture, and its exquisite cuisine including sushi and ramen. Despite limited natural resources, Japan has developed into the world's third-largest economy through innovation and manufacturing excellence.
