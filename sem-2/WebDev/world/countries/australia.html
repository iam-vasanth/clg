Australia

Australia is both a country and a continent, located in the Southern Hemisphere between the Pacific and Indian Oceans. It is the world's sixth-largest country by total area and is known for its unique wildlife, including kangaroos, koalas, and platypuses found nowhere else on Earth. Australia's landscape is diverse, featuring the iconic Outback, the Great Barrier Reef (the world's largest coral reef system), pristine beaches, and modern cosmopolitan cities. The country has a strong economy based on natural resources, agriculture, and services. Major cities include Sydney, known for its Opera House and Harbour Bridge, Melbourne with its cultural scene, and Canberra, the capital. Australia has a rich Indigenous heritage dating back over 65,000 years, making Aboriginal Australians one of the world's oldest continuous cultures. The country is also known for its outdoor lifestyle, sports culture, and high quality of life.
