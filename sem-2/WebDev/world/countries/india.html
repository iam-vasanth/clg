India

India, officially the Republic of India, is a vast South Asian country with a rich and diverse cultural heritage spanning over 5,000 years. As the world's largest democracy and most populous nation, India is home to more than 1.4 billion people representing thousands of ethnic groups, languages, and religious traditions. The country's name derives from the Indus River, one of the major river systems that has sustained civilizations in the region for millennia.
